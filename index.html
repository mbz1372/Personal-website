About Me

I am a Product Manager and Business Operator with a strong background in hospitality, travel-tech, and marketplace operations. My career started from the operational side of the hotel industry, where I learned how pricing, supply management, customer experience, and B2B partnerships fundamentally shape revenue performance. Over time, this operational foundation became my biggest advantage as I transitioned into product and business ownership roles.

For the past several years, I have led large-scale supply operations and product initiatives at IranHotelOnline, one of the largest hospitality platforms in Iran, managing a portfolio of 1,400+ hotels, complex pricing systems, capacity workflows, and provider integrations. Working directly with founders and cross-functional teams, I helped design and implement scalable processes, CRM systems, dashboards, and automation tools that improved efficiency, reduced operational friction, and boosted revenue.

In parallel, I co-founded a startup focused on delivering tailored digital solutions for businesses, where I collaborated closely with clients, defined product requirements, and ensured end-to-end execution from concept to launch.

My work sits at the intersection of product, data, and operations. I excel in environments where deep problem-solving, cross-team collaboration, structured execution, and ownership are required. Today, I am open to remote Product Manager and Business Operations roles, especially in travel, marketplace, SaaS, or operationally intensive businesses.
